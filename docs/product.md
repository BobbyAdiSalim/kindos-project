# Product Description – Healthcare Appointment & Care Matching Platform for Deaf Users

## 1. Product Overview

The platform is a digital healthcare solution designed for deaf and hard of hearing patients. The use is to connect patients with verified doctors for both virtual and in-person consultations. Patients can complete a needs-based questionnaire, find doctors based on availability, and location, and manage appointments from start to finish. Doctors can manage profiles, availability, appointments, and communicate with patients, while system administrators oversee verification, quality, and analytics.

The platform’s designed to reduce barriers for deaf patients to access healthcare while streamlining scheduling and communication for doctors, using **text-based accessibility features** to ensure all users can interact fully without relying on auditory cues.

## 2. Target Users

### Deaf Patients

* Individuals seeking accessible medical consultations
* Users who prefer quick access to appropriate care without complex navigation
* Users who value clear, transparent, and text-based communication for notifications, instructions, and messaging

### Doctors

* Licensed healthcare professionals able to communicate effectively with deaf patients through text
* Doctors looking for efficient scheduling tools and access to underserved patient groups
* Providers offering both virtual and in-person care options

### System Administrators

* Operators maintaining platform quality, accessibility, and compliance
* Stakeholders monitoring usage, growth, and performance metrics

## 3. Market Sizing (High-Level)

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

* Over 400 million people worldwide have hearing loss, with growing demand for accessible digital healthcare.
* Options for treatment has surged in recent year, expanding opportunities for accessible platforms.

### Serviceable Available Market (SAM)

* Deaf patients who use digital platforms to find and book healthcare
* Doctors capable of providing accessible consultations via text

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

* Early adopters: tech-savvy deaf patients in urban areas and clinics/doctors offering virtual care with text accessibility
* Expansion through community networks, advocacy, and referrals

## 4. User Research Insights

* Patients face communication barriers and difficulty accessing information through traditional auditory channels.
* Finding doctors who cater to deaf patients is often difficult and time consuming.
* Doctors require tools to communicate clearly with patients using text.
* Trust and accessibility are critical: users expect verified doctors, clear notifications, and secure authentication.

These insights informed the prioritization of **core text-based accessibility and booking flows**, followed by communication and discovery enhancements.

## 5. Feature Prioritization

### Priority 1 – Must Have

**Authentication & Access**

* Single entry page for registration or login (1)
* Secure login and password reset (2)

**Patient Core Flow**

* Patient registration and profile management (3, 4)
* Needs questionnaire and care type selection (5)
* Doctor listing filtered by care type and availability (6)
* Doctor profile viewing (8)
* View doctor availability and time slots (9)
* Book appointments (virtual/in-person) with confirmation/reminder emails (10)
* View and manage appointments (11)

**Doctor Core Flow**

* Doctor registration with professional details and verification submission (15)
* Doctor profile management (16)
* Availability and scheduling setup (17)
* View schedule and patient details for appointments (18)

**Administration & Trust**

* Admin login (23)
* Doctor verification and approval (24)

### Priority 2 – Should Have

**Discovery Enhancements**

* Map-based doctor discovery using GPS (7)

**Communication**

* Patient–doctor messaging (12)
* Doctor–patient chat (20)

**Scheduling Flexibility**

* Doctor reschedule/cancel appointments (19)
* Patient waitlist and cancellation notifications (14)

**Care Continuity**

* Doctor view of patient appointment history and summaries (21)
* Doctor writing appointment summaries (22)

### Priority 3 – Nice to Have

**Social Proof & Feedback**

* Patient ratings and reviews (13)

**Analytics & Optimization**

* Admin booking analytics and trends dashboard (25)

## 6. Summary

This platform delivers an **accessible, secure, and reliable healthcare booking experience for deaf patients**, ensuring trust through verified doctors and usability through **text-based accessibility features**. Future releases will enhance communication, discovery, and analytics to increase accesibility and improve patient experience.
